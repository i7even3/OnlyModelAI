Frequently Asked Questions OnlyModelAI

Getting Started

  • Do I need followers, tech skills, or experience?
    No. OnlyModelAI is designed for complete beginners. You do not need an audience, coding knowledge, or design background. If you are curious and willing to follow instructions, you can begin.
  • What exactly will I create?
    You will build your own AI powered persona that can be used to publish content, engage with an audience, and support your own online activity. The program includes prompts, templates, and tool instructions.
  • How long does the program take?
    The roadmap is structured as a 14-day plan, but you can progress at your own pace. Some users see initial results within the first week.

Plans and Access

  • What is the difference between the Starter and Deluxe options?
    The Starter plan is a monthly subscription that provides access to all tools, written guides, and community support.
    The Deluxe option is a single payment that grants lifetime access to all materials, including any bonus content.
  • What happens if I cancel my subscription?
    Access to private content and community features will be removed within 48 hours of cancellation. You may rejoin at any time by resubscribing.
  • Is there a refund policy?
    A refund is available within 14 days if no digital materials have been accessed. Once any content is used, the purchase becomes non refundable.
