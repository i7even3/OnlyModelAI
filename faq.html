Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need followers, skills, or experience?

No. OnlyModelAI is designed for total beginners. You don’t need any followers, tech skills, or experience to get started. Everything is explained clearly, step by step.

What’s included in Starter vs. Deluxe?

The Starter plan includes the full 14-day launch roadmap, basic tools, and prompt templates. The Deluxe plan gives you everything from Starter plus exclusive bonus strategies, prompt archives, a private access key, and content creation extras.

Do I lose access if I stop using it?

No. Both Starter and Deluxe are one time purchases. There’s no subscription or expiration. You keep access to everything you’ve paid for, forever. This does not take discord support into account or any support.

Is this platform safe and legal?

OnlyModelAI is an educational resource. We don’t sell or promote illegal content. It’s your responsibility to follow platform guidelines and use the materials ethically.

Can I resell or share the content?

No. Each purchase comes with a personal license. Redistribution, resale, or sharing of materials will result in immediate and permanent access removal.
