Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need followers, skills, or experience?

No. OnlyModelAI is built for beginners and also for those who tried before and got stuck. You do not need followers, tech knowledge, or past experience. Everything is explained step by step.

What is the difference between Starter, Deluxe, and Mentorship?

Starter includes the 14 day launch guide, basic tools, and posting templates.
Deluxe includes everything in Starter plus strategy packs, prompt archive access, advanced monetization, and the full content stack.
Mentorship adds one on one coaching, a custom audit and growth plan, and priority support.

Is this a subscription?

No. All plans are one time payments. You keep full access to what you purchased. Updates and Telegram access are included, but access to future support channels may change over time.

Is OnlyModelAI legal and safe to use?

Yes. OnlyModelAI is an educational system. It does not include or promote illegal content. You must follow the rules of each platform when posting or messaging.

Can I share the content with others?

No. Each purchase is for personal use only. Sharing, reselling, or redistributing the content will remove your access to future updates and support.
